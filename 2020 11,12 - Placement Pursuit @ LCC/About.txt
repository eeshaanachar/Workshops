Placement Pursuit is a series of podcasts by LCC-SJCE. The scripts for the same were after surveying the members of the core committee of the club. In the first season (created by Akashraj, Eeshaan, Surya Kamal, and Sharanya), there were a total of 6 episodes. The first episode was aired on November 21, 2020, and the last episode was aired on December 17, 2020. All the episodes can be found at https://www.instagram.com/linuxcampusclub/
