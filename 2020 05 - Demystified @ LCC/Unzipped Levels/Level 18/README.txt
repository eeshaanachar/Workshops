The given puzzle is a substitution cipher in which one letter stands for another.

If you are given that X equals O, it will equal O throughout the puzzle. 
Single letter, short words and words using an apostrophe give you clues in locating vowels. 
Solution is a meaningful sentence. 
And the password is all small letters with no spaces between those words of the solution.

Do a dictionary attack with your brain.
